Jacquemus:
Accordion Book

Jacquemus: Accordion Book is based on highlighting the history, works, inspirations, and contributions of a specific designer. The designer chosen is Simon Porte Jacquemus and the pages outline Jacquemus’ fashion brand and individual collections. The pages take inspiration from Jacquemus’ signature feminine French style, while highlighting his ethos and influences in the page design.
