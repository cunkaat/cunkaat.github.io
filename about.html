Bio

Kat Cunanan is a third-year student studying design and computer science at the University of San Francisco. She is currently interning at Perform for Life LLC and Perform for Golf, exploring the opportunities of a design and marketing intern with responsibilities of visual communication, marketing tools, and brand identity.

She was born in San Francisco and raised in Manila. Her upbringing has contributed towards her personal style and ethos of design, merging international influences and tastes. She has personal interests in fashion, film, and music, which all flood into her work and design.
